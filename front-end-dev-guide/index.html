Front-End Developer Guide

This guide is intended primarily for developers who are experienced in programming but are not well-versed at writing code for web user interfaces.

The information contained in this guide is the result of ten years of web development, in particular, on the ASP.NET stack, and is filtered through my understanding of accepted paradigms and practices. Ultimately, the information is a reflection of my opinions and others whom I have sought for clarification.
