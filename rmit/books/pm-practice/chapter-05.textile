h1. Chapter 5 - Scheduling the Project

The three activities: planning, budgeting and scheduling are inter-related.

* _Budgeting_: amounts and timings of resources received or expended depend on scheduling of tasks
* _Scheduling_: when and how long tasks are expected to run depend on the overall project plan
* _Planning_: what to plan for depends on estimated size of budget and estimated resources and proposed milestones and deadlines
